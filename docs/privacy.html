Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 13, 2026

Overview

PANOMI is a free, open-source unified game launcher that helps you organize and launch games from multiple platforms. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

PANOMI does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

All data is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Game library information (detected from installed launchers)
  • Application settings and preferences
  • Game icons and metadata

Data Storage

All application data is stored in your local AppData folder:

%LOCALAPPDATA%\Panomi

You can delete this folder at any time to remove all PANOMI data from your device.

Network Access

PANOMI may access the internet only for:

  • Checking for application updates (GitHub releases)
  • Opening web links when you click on them in the app

No personal data is transmitted during these operations.

Third-Party Services

PANOMI reads game information from launchers you have installed (Steam, Epic Games, GOG, etc.) but does not communicate with these services directly. PANOMI only reads locally stored data.

Affiliate Disclosure

Some links in this app may be affiliate links, which help support PANOMI at no extra cost to you. As an Amazon Associate, I earn from qualifying purchases.

Sponsored Content

Some items displayed in PANOMI are from partners who support the app's development. These are clearly marked as "Sponsored" and are shown based on partnership agreements, not your personal data or usage patterns. PANOMI does not track or profile users for advertising purposes.

Independence Disclaimer

PANOMI is an independent application and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any third-party software, services, or platforms referenced within the app. All trademarks and logos are the property of their respective owners.

PANOMI does not host, distribute, or modify any third-party software or content. All applications are launched from your local system, and all external links open in your default browser.

Children's Privacy

PANOMI does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.

Open Source

PANOMI is open-source software. The source code is available at github.com/dorsaljr/PANOMI.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
